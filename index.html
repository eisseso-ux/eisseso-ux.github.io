Artist Bio

Olivia Eisses is a multidisciplinary artist whose practice spans sculpture, photography, plush objects, wearable work, and 2D design. Her work explores texture, memory, identity, and the handmade object through experimental material combinations and narrative composition.

She develops projects that move between fine art and functional design, often blending playful forms with technically detailed construction. Her portfolio reflects an ongoing interest in tactile storytelling, visual rhythm, and emotionally resonant image-making.
