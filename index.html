Artist Bio

Olivia is an artist and creative with a passion for the strange and wonderful. She received a BA in Studio Art from Wheaton College, but has been making, designing, and crafting her entire life. Her skills portfolio is broad and she is always learning new processes.

She works part time as an artist, taking commissions and selling things you should absolutely go buy on her Etsy shop.

In her art she loves balancing the comforting and the creepy. She creates dreams with just a dash of nightmare to keep you grounded (or sometimes the opposite). Themes in Oliviaâ€™s work include dreams, the awkwardness and grace of the human body, other-wordly creatures, storytelling and nonsense. She deeply loves people, and uses her art to connect people to each other, the world around them, and most of all themselves. She is constantly looking for ways to use art to serve her community. She firmly believes the act of creating changes people, increasing both vulnerability and resiliency. As such she is always looking to help people discover their artistic capabilities and to show them that have something worthwhile to say.
